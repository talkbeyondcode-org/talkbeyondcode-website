Agents flattened the output gap. A motivated junior with a good setup now ships volume that used to signal experience. So what is senior actually for?

Our take: the ladder moved from output to judgment. Knowing what not to build. Smelling the design that will hurt in six months. Owning the incident at 2am. None of that is something you can prompt your way into yet.

If you are early in your career, the move is not to out-type the agent. It is to get reps on the decisions it cannot make for you.

This is a starting draft. We'll flesh out what we look for when we promote.
