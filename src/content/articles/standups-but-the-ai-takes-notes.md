We let an agent run our standup notes for a month. It was the most human ritual we had, so automating it felt slightly wrong from day one.

Some of it worked. The summary was decent, action items got captured, and nobody had to be the scribe. Some of it did not. The notes flattened the disagreements that were usually the useful part.

We kept the note-taking and quietly turned off the parts that tried to summarize how people felt.

This is a starting draft. We'll add the before-and-after of our actual standup format.
