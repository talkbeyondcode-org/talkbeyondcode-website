If every candidate has an agent, the take-home stops testing what it used to. So what are you actually measuring?

We rethought our loop around the assumption that AI is in the room. The interesting signal is no longer "can you produce code." It is "can you explain it, defend it, and catch where it is wrong."

The piece covers what we changed, what we kept, and the questions that still separate people once the agent is allowed to help.

This is a starting draft. We'll publish the actual prompts we now use.
