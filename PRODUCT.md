# PRODUCT.md — TalkBeyondCode

register: brand

## Product purpose

The marketing/content home for **TalkBeyondCode**, a media brand run by four working software engineers. It is the hub for their flagship podcast **Ctrl+Shift+AI**, their articles, and their creator profiles. The site's job is to *communicate a point of view* and convert curious engineers into listeners/readers/subscribers — the design itself is the product.

## Users

Working software engineers — junior to staff+, across stacks and companies. They are technically literate, allergic to marketing fluff, and skeptical of AI hype. They've seen a thousand dev-tool landing pages and a thousand AI-podcast thumbnails. They respect craft and honesty; they bounce off slogans.

## Brand voice

Three physical-object words: **honest, mechanical, unpolished.** Like an engineer explaining how something actually works at a whiteboard after the meeting ended — precise, a little raw, no slide deck.

- Authentic, practical, insightful, engineer-to-engineer.
- Anti-hype. "No scripts. No gatekeeping. Just engineers talking to engineers."
- Curiosity over authority. Real experience over generic advice.

## Strategic principles

- **Communicate, not transact.** It's a content brand, not a SaaS signup funnel.
- **The medium is conversation + audio.** Sound is literally the product (podcast). The design can make sound visible.
- **Earned technicality.** This is a genuine engineering brand, so technical/instrument visual language (monospace as structure, spec layouts, signal readouts) is honest here, not costume.

## Anti-references (what to NOT look like)

- The generic **AI-podcast / dev-tool landing page**: near-black background, cyan→violet→magenta neon gradient, gradient text, glowing orbs, three identical feature cards. This is the exact category reflex and it contradicts the anti-hype voice.
- Hype-deck energy: huge gradient claims, "supercharge", rocket emojis.
- Crypto-neon. Glassmorphism. Centered icon-title-subtitle card stacks.

## Distinctive hook

The brand identity is a **keyboard shortcut**: `Ctrl + Shift + AI`. The keycap motif is the ownable idea. Lean into keyboards, terminals, and engineering instruments as the visual world.
